Our unwavering commitment to quality, transparency, and nutritional excellence drives every aspect of our comprehensive operations.

We specialize in the strategic sourcing, professional processing, and reliable supply of premium agricultural staples including cowpeas, groundnuts, and diverse quality grains. Agricultural Staples & Grains

Premium quality grains and legumes sourced from Nigeria's finest agricultural regions, meeting international export standards.

  • Cowpeas (Black-eyed Peas) FAO Grade 1
    Local white, brown, and red varieties with moisture content <12%. High protein content (22-25%) and fiber-rich composition.
    Applications: Human consumption, livestock feed, food processing | Export Capacity: 500-2000MT per shipment
  • Premium Groundnuts (Peanuts) Oil 45-50%
    Spanish, Virginia, and Runner varieties. Aflatoxin levels <4ppb. Available raw, blanched, or roasted.
    Applications: Oil extraction, confectionery, direct consumption | Storage: Temperature-controlled facilities
  • Soybeans Protein 22-25%
    High-protein legume rich in amino acids, perfect for food processing, animal feed, and oil extraction.
    Applications: Food processing, animal feed, oil production | Quality: Export-grade standards
  • Premium Millet Varieties Gluten-Free
    Pearl, finger, and foxtail millet varieties. Premium grade with foreign matter <2%. High calcium and iron content.
    Applications: Flour production, brewing, health foods | Certifications: Organic standards available
  • Maize (Corn) Moisture 13-14%
    White dent, yellow dent, and flint corn varieties. Broken kernels <3%. Multiple processing options available.
    Applications: Animal feed, human consumption, industrial processing | Capacity: 5000MT monthly
  • Sorghum & Rice Varieties Premium Grade
    White and red sorghum, premium rice varieties. Drought-resistant crops with consistent quality standards.
    Applications: Brewing, flour production, direct consumption | Processing: Clean, graded, sorted

Nutritional Blends & Processed Foods

Advanced nutritional products and therapeutic food blends designed for optimal health outcomes and specialized dietary requirements.

  • Tom Brown Multi-Grain Blend Protein 15-18%
    Premium blend of maize, millet, sorghum, soybeans, and groundnuts. Fortified with iron, zinc, vitamin A, and B-complex vitamins.
    Applications: Infant nutrition, school feeding programs, therapeutic nutrition | Shelf Life: 12 months
  • Custom Nutritional Formulations WHO Standards
    Tailored nutritional blends for specific requirements. Specialized formulations for vulnerable groups including pregnant women, children, and elderly.

Premium Fertilizers & Agricultural Inputs

Superior-grade fertilizers and agricultural inputs designed to maximize crop yields and promote sustainable farming practices.

  • DAP (Diammonium Phosphate) 18-46-0
    High-grade phosphate fertilizer with 18% Nitrogen and 46% Phosphate. Granule size 2-4mm, moisture content <2%, highly water-soluble.
    Applications: Base application for all crops, phosphorus-deficient soils | Export Capacity: 1000+MT bulk shipments
  • Premium Urea Fertilizer 46% Nitrogen
    Prilled and granular urea with 46% nitrogen content. Uniform size distribution 2-4mm, biuret content <1.5%, minimal dust formation.
    Applications: Quick nitrogen release, rapid plant growth | Quality: Free-flowing granules, moisture-protected packaging
  • Granular Sulfur 99.8% Purity
    Ultra-pure granular sulfur, 2-6mm uniform sizing, bright yellow appearance, dust content <0.5%. Slowly available sulfur for extended crop nutrition.
    Applications: Soil pH reduction, sulfur deficiency correction, fungicidal properties
  • Balanced NPK Formulations Multiple Grades
    Standard blends (15-15-15, 20-10-10, 10-20-20) and custom formulations. Enhanced options with magnesium and slow-release coating technology.
    Applications: General crop production, specialized agricultural systems | Options: Soil-specific, crop-specific blends
  • Specialized Potassium & Micronutrients Premium Grade
    Muriate of Potash (60% K2O), Sulfate of Potash, Zinc Sulfate (33% Zn), Iron Chelate, Manganese Sulfate, and custom micronutrient blends.
    Applications: Root development, fruit quality, enzyme activation, deficiency correction
